Bacon

Bacon is one of the most famous cuts of meat. This particular cut comes from the side of the pig. When social media took over bacon quickly became popular. This craze has led to an International Bacon Day and many other festivals such as the "Bacon Takedown" in Brooklyn is just one example of these festivals. Foods such as the BLT, bacon cheeseburger, cobb salad, and bacon ice crean have been created from this craze. Bacon is a really tasty cut of meat. However, it does not cost as much as other cuts from the hog.

Crown Roast

The crown roast is not a cheap piece of meat. This piece of meat retails for about $6.99. However, the look of the cut is why it is so expensive. The cut is not called the crown roast for nothing. It is shaped like a crown. This is the meal for the king or queen roaster at a roast fest. A roast fest is usually hosted by teenagers or young adults to criticize one another and they vote on who is the king or queen based on their roasts. The winner will then put this cut on their head proclaiming they are the king or queen of roasting. After the group will cook the cut of meat and have a good time eating the delicacy.

Ribs

Ribs are another good choice of meat. Hogs can make spare ribs, St Louis ribs, or baby back ribs. The most famous of these is the baby back ribs. This is primarily because Chili's released an ad campaign for their famous baby back ribs. Most notably of this ad campaign was the jingle that they released with their commercial. The other style ribs are also a good choice as well.
